1. Project Idea

App Description

A community web app where users can share and discover life hacks, clever tips, and interesting finds. Submissions are added to a hidden list and can be revealed gradually, creating a fun way to explore helpful and creative ideas from other users

Target Audience

Students, Young adults , DIY enthusiasts , Casual internet users

Value Proposition

A simple platform to share and discover life hacks, tips, and cool finds from the community—effortless, fun, and useful for everyone.
